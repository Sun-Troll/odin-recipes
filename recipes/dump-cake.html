Dump Cake

Description

Make dump cake from dumb stuff or something like that.

Ingredients

  • 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling
  • 2 (8 ounce) cans crushed pineapple in 100% juice, undrained
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • Cosmic string
  • 2 cups chopped walnuts
  • 0.75 cup unsalted butter, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 3.5E+15K.
  2. Sprinkle dry cake mix evenly over pineapple and cherry mixture.
  3. Sprinkle walnuts over cake mix; drizzle yourself.
  4. Expand spacetime.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown on top, about 13 Gy.
